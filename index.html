90's burger

Burger resturant located in nablus. Best beef and best chicken burgers in the erea.

It's called 90's burger because it gives the vibe of the 90's tv shows and movies characters.

We serve burgers, fries, and drinks. Our specialty is the 90's burger, which is made with fresh beef, sauce, and buns.

We also have a specialty burger made with chicken, which is served with a side of fries and a drink.

Our menu is always changing, so don't hesitate to check it out.

We have two branches; one in Alaa aldeen street, and the second one is in Rafidia, Blaiblah street.
